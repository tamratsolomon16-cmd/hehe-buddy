Roller.py is built on the interactive pygame  and random libraries. Roller.py is a fun die roller that can roll up to 6 at a time. 

Upon being run, Roller.py will open an 800*500 window with six dice in their starting positions (each having a value of 1). 
By pressing the "R" key the user can reroll all unfrozen dice. 
How to freeze (lock) dice? Fret not,
All you have to look at the die you wish to lock its position (from the left) and press the number which corresponds to it (starts from one).
To freeze the third die, for example, you would press the number 3 on your keyboard and that will prevent it from being rolled until you press 3 again.
You can freeze multiple dice (you can even freeze all of them!). 
Pressing Q will quit the program
